## A17 – Discover 10 different types of locks in use

### Objective

Identify ten different types of physical locks used in daily life.

---

### Findings

* Deadbolt locks are commonly used on main doors because they provide strong security by extending a solid metal bolt deep into the door frame, making forced entry more difficult.

* Padlocks are versatile and portable locks used for securing gates, lockers, and storage units. They can be easily attached and removed, making them suitable for temporary or flexible use.

* Knob locks are built into door handles and are commonly used on interior doors. While convenient, they offer lower security compared to other lock types.

* Lever handle locks are often used in commercial buildings because they are easier to operate, especially for people with disabilities, while still providing basic security.

* Mortise locks are installed inside the door and have a more complex internal mechanism, making them stronger and more secure than standard locks.

* Cam locks are small locks used in cabinets, drawers, and mailboxes. They use a rotating metal piece to lock and unlock, providing basic protection for small storage areas.

* Rim locks are mounted on the surface of doors and are often used in older buildings. They are easy to install and visible from the inside.

* Cylinder locks use a key-operated cylinder mechanism and are widely used in modern doors. The key turns the cylinder to lock or unlock the door.

* Chain locks are used as an additional security feature on doors, allowing the door to be opened slightly while still being secured from the inside.

* Combination locks use a sequence of numbers instead of a key to unlock. They are commonly used in lockers and safes, removing the need to carry physical keys.

---

### Mitigations

Using strong, high-quality locks, combining multiple locking mechanisms, and maintaining them regularly improves overall security.

---

### Reflection

This task showed that different locks serve different purposes, and combining them provides stronger protection.
