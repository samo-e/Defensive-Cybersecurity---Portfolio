## A2 – Discover security concepts used in public space

### Objective

Identify five different security concepts used in public spaces (e.g., malls, airports, streets). The goal is to understand how safety is maintained using different security strategies and systems.

---

### Findings

* Surveillance using CCTV cameras is widely used in public places such as shopping centres and transport stations. These cameras continuously record activities and allow security personnel to monitor situations in real time or review footage later. This helps in identifying incidents and discouraging criminal behaviour.

* Access control is used in restricted areas like staff-only zones or airport sections. It ensures that only authorised individuals can enter certain spaces using tickets, passes, or ID verification, helping to reduce unauthorised access.

* Security screening is commonly seen in airports and large events. People and their belongings are checked using scanners or manual inspection to detect prohibited or dangerous items before entry.

* Visible security presence, such as guards or police officers, plays an important role in public safety. Their presence alone can discourage crime and allows for quick response if an incident occurs.

* Proper lighting in public areas is an important environmental security concept. Well-lit streets and parking areas reduce hiding spots and make it easier for people and cameras to observe surroundings.

---

### Mitigations
Public safety can be improved by combining multiple security concepts instead of relying on just one method. Regular maintenance of cameras, lighting, and screening equipment is important to ensure they function properly. Training security personnel improves response effectiveness, and encouraging the public to report suspicious behaviour can further strengthen overall security.

---

### Reflection

This task showed that public safety depends on a combination of different security concepts working together. No single method is enough on its own, and layered security provides better protection.
