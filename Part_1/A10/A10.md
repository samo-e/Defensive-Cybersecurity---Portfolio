## A10 – Discover privacy techniques used offline

### Objective

Identify five different offline privacy techniques used in daily life.

---

### Findings

* Shredding documents helps prevent sensitive information from being recovered after disposal by cutting paper into small pieces.

* Locking personal items in drawers or safes protects private documents from unauthorised access.

* Privacy screens limit viewing angles on devices, preventing others from seeing the screen in public places.

* Covering keypads while entering PINs helps prevent others from observing sensitive information.

* Speaking in private or lowering voice protects conversations from being overheard.

---

### Mitigations (high-level recommendations)

Using multiple techniques together, staying aware of surroundings, and developing good habits can significantly improve offline privacy protection.

---

### Reflection

This task showed that privacy protection is not only digital but also depends on everyday physical actions and awareness.
