## A23 – Enhance the cybersecurity at your home

For this activity, I worked on improving cybersecurity at home by getting my housemates to install McAfee on their phones. The idea was to make sure everyone had at least some basic level of protection on their personal devices, since phones are used for things like banking, emails, and social media.

After installing it, I explained to them how it helps in detecting harmful apps, blocking unsafe websites, and warning about potential security risks. Before this, most of them didn’t really have any proper security tools on their phones, so this was a simple but useful improvement.

It wasn’t anything very advanced, but it still made a difference because now everyone has some level of protection and is a bit more aware of cybersecurity. Overall, it showed me that even small steps can help improve security in everyday life.
