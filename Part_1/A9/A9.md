## A9 – Discover privacy techniques used online

### Objective

Identify five different privacy techniques used online to protect personal information and user activity.

---

### Findings

* Using a VPN helps protect user privacy by encrypting internet traffic and hiding the user’s IP address, making it harder to track online activity.

* Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security by requiring a second verification step, reducing the risk of account compromise.

* Using strong and unique passwords for different accounts prevents attackers from accessing multiple services if one password is leaked.

* Adjusting privacy settings on websites allows users to control what information is shared publicly and with third parties.

* Private browsing mode prevents the browser from storing history, cookies, and temporary data on the device.

---

### Mitigations

Online privacy can be improved by combining multiple techniques such as VPNs, strong passwords, and 2FA. Users should regularly review privacy settings and avoid sharing sensitive information unnecessarily.

---

### Reflection

This task showed that protecting privacy online requires both tools and responsible user behaviour.
