## A14 – Discover 5 AI-enabled security solutions

### Objective

Identify five AI-based security solutions used in modern systems. I looked at tools like intrusion detection, facial recognition, fraud detection, email filtering, and smart surveillance, as these are the areas where AI is being used most visibly in security today.

---

### Findings

* **AI-based intrusion detection systems**

  AI-based intrusion detection systems monitor network traffic and analyse patterns to detect unusual or suspicious behaviour. Unlike traditional systems, AI can learn from past data and improve its ability to detect new types of attacks.

  I found this useful because normal rule-based systems often miss new patterns. AI makes detection more flexible, especially in large networks where manual checking is not possible all the time.

* **AI-powered facial recognition systems**

  AI-powered facial recognition systems analyse facial features such as distance between eyes or shape of the face to identify individuals. These systems are used in airports, offices, and smartphones for secure and quick authentication.

  In my view, this is convenient but also a bit sensitive from a privacy angle. It is fast, yes, but it should be used carefully with proper consent and strong data protection.

* **AI-based fraud detection systems**

  AI-based fraud detection systems are widely used by banks and online payment platforms. They analyse transaction patterns in real time and detect unusual activities, such as large or unexpected purchases, to prevent fraud.

  This is especially important because fraud often happens very quickly. AI can flag strange behaviour much faster than a human analyst, which helps stop losses before they grow.

* **AI-powered email security systems**

  AI-powered email security systems scan emails for phishing attempts by analysing content, links, and sender behaviour. They can identify patterns that may not be obvious to humans, improving detection accuracy.

  I noticed this is very useful because phishing emails often look normal at first. AI helps catch small signs, like weird phrasing or suspicious sender behaviour, which people can easily miss.

* **Smart surveillance cameras**

  Smart surveillance cameras use AI to detect motion, recognise faces, and identify suspicious behaviour automatically. They can send alerts to users without needing constant human monitoring.

  I think this is one of the best examples of AI in physical security, because one operator can monitor many cameras at once. Still, false alerts can happen, so human review is still needed.

---

### Mitigations

Combining AI with human monitoring, updating models, and protecting user data improves system reliability and effectiveness. AI tools should not be left alone without review, because they can make mistakes or miss edge cases.

It is also important to check for bias and privacy issues, especially in facial recognition and surveillance systems. Good security means not only accurate detection, but also responsible use of data.

---

### Reflection

This task showed that AI improves security by automating detection but still requires human oversight. It is good at analysing large amounts of data, but it is not perfect on its own.

I also realised that AI security tools are most useful when they support people rather than replace them. The human part still matters a lot, especially when deciding whether an alert is real or not.
