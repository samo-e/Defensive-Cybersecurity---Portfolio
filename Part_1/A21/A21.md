## A21 – Participate in an online cybersecurity discussion

For this activity, I scheduled and attended a short online video call with a cybersecurity professional named Aishwarya K. through the platform Topmate. The session lasted for around 15 minutes and focused on getting a general understanding of the cybersecurity field in 2026.

During the discussion, she gave an overview of how the cybersecurity industry is evolving, especially with the rise of AI, cloud computing, and increasing cyber threats. She explained that cybersecurity is becoming more dynamic, and professionals need to constantly update their skills to stay relevant. We also talked about what new developers should focus on if they want to build a career in cybersecurity. She emphasised the importance of having strong fundamentals, understanding real-world threats, and gaining practical experience instead of just theoretical knowledge.

She also mentioned that in 2026, areas like cloud security, AI in security, and threat detection are becoming more important. According to her, staying curious, continuously learning, and building hands-on projects are key factors for success in this field.

Overall, this discussion was really helpful because it gave me a clearer idea of what the cybersecurity field currently looks like and what skills I should focus on moving forward. It also made me realise that cybersecurity is not just about tools, but also about thinking like an attacker and constantly adapting to new challenges.
