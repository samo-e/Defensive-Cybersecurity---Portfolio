## A19 – Join a CS/DS/cybersecurity club

I recently joined Coders for Causes (CFC) at UWA, and it’s been a pretty good experience so far. The club is focused on building software for non-profit organisations, which makes it a bit different from typical coding clubs because the work actually has real impact.

From what I’ve seen, CFC gives students the chance to work on real projects in teams, which is useful for gaining practical experience outside of uni assignments. It also helps in improving skills like teamwork, communication, and working with real clients, not just coding. Overall, joining the club feels like a good step towards getting more hands-on experience and preparing for future internships or jobs.
