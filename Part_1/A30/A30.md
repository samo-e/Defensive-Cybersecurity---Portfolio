## A30 - Complete an online cybersecurity module

I completed the Cyber Security for Small Business short course provided by Australian Taxation Office through their online platform. I chose to do this course because I’m interested in starting my own business in the future, and I wanted to understand the basic security risks involved.

The course covered simple but important concepts like protecting business data, using strong passwords, recognising scams, and keeping systems updated. It was more focused on practical advice rather than technical details, which made it easy to understand and apply. Overall, it gave me a good starting point and made me realise that cybersecurity is something that should be considered from the very beginning when setting up a business.

Course Link: https://smallbusiness.taxsuperandyou.gov.au/cybersecurity
