## A20 - Participate in a discussion with your friends about cybersecurity event

For this activity, I had a discussion with my friends about a recent cybersecurity incident at University of Western Australia. We talked about what actually happened, how the attack could have occurred, and what kind of impact it had on students and staff, especially regarding password security and system access.

During the discussion, we also shared our own thoughts on how such incidents can be prevented, like using stronger passwords, enabling multi-factor authentication, and being more careful with suspicious links or emails. It was a pretty useful conversation because everyone had slightly different ideas and experiences, so it helped us understand the topic better from multiple perspectives.

I have meeting minutes and some pictures from the discussion as evidence to show that the activity was completed. Overall, it was a simple but effective way to learn more about real-world cybersecurity issues in a relatable setting.
