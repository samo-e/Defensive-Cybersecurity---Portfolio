## A14 – Discover 5 AI-enabled security solutions

### Objective

Identify five different AI-enabled security solutions used to protect systems, data, and people. The goal is to understand how artificial intelligence improves security by detecting threats, analysing patterns, and responding automatically.

---

### Method

1. Defined scope:

* Focused on security tools that use AI or machine learning.
* Included both physical and cybersecurity solutions.

2. Research approach:

* Looked at real-world uses of AI in security (homes, companies, online systems).
* Selected tools with different use cases.

3. Selection criteria:

* Must use AI or machine learning.
* Each solution must perform a different type of security task.

---

### Findings

#### 1. AI-based Intrusion Detection System (IDS)

* Used in networks to detect cyber attacks.

**How it works:**

* Monitors network traffic.
* Uses AI to detect unusual patterns or behaviour.

**Weakness:**

* May give false alerts (false positives).
* Needs training data to be effective.

---

#### 2. AI-powered Facial Recognition Security

* Used in airports, phones, and secure buildings.

**How it works:**

* Uses AI to analyse facial features.
* Matches faces with stored data for identification.

**Weakness:**

* Privacy concerns.
* Can be less accurate in poor lighting or with changes in appearance.

---

#### 3. AI-based Fraud Detection Systems

* Used by banks and online payment systems.

**How it works:**

* Analyses transaction patterns.
* Detects unusual or suspicious activity in real time.

**Weakness:**

* May block legitimate transactions.
* Requires large amounts of data.

---

#### 4. AI-powered Email Phishing Detection

* Used by email services.

**How it works:**

* Scans email content and behaviour.
* Uses AI to detect phishing attempts and scams.

**Weakness:**

* Advanced phishing emails may still pass through.

---

#### 5. AI Smart Surveillance Cameras

* Used in public places and businesses.

**How it works:**

* Uses AI to detect motion, faces, or suspicious behaviour.
* Can send alerts automatically.

**Weakness:**

* Expensive compared to normal cameras.
* Raises privacy concerns.

---

### Mitigations (high-level recommendations)

1. Combine AI with human monitoring:

* Humans can review and confirm AI decisions.

2. Regularly update AI models:

* Keeps detection accurate and effective.

3. Protect user privacy:

* Follow rules when collecting and using data.

4. Test and tune systems:

* Reduce false positives and improve performance.

---

### Reflection

This task showed that AI is becoming a big part of modern security. It helps detect threats faster and handle large amounts of data. However, AI is not perfect and can make mistakes. It works best when combined with human judgement and proper system management.
